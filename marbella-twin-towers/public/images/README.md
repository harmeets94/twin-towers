## About Section
- `about.webp` - About section main image

## Why Choose Us

## Floor Plans (PNG format recommended)
- `f1.png` - Floor Plan A
- `f2.png` - Floor Plan B
- `f3.png` - Floor Plan C
- `f4.png` - Floor Plan D
- `f5.png` - Floor Plan E

## Gallery (WebP format recommended)
- `g1.webp` through `g11.webp` - Gallery images

## Logo
- `logo.png` - Site logo (recommended size: 200x80px)

## Open Graph
- `og-image.jpg` - Social media share image (1200x630px)

## Image Optimization Tips
1. Use WebP format for photos (better compression)
2. Use PNG for logos and floor plans (transparency support)
3. Recommended hero image size: 1920x1080px
4. Recommended gallery image size: 1200x800px
5. Recommended floor plan size: 1600x1200px
